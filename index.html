Sergio Yuppie

The king of downhill. This is how Sergio Yuppie is known around the world. In Brazil Yuppie, the slope destroyer, is a legendary figure and the greatest champion of the sport in Brazil. His aggressive way of walking, giving slides on every inch of asphalt and without losing gas, makes him a reference. A native of São Paulo, capital Yuppie has built his story on the skateboard scene since 1986. The guy is a true legend in the sport and lives up to the title he receives: the downhill king.
